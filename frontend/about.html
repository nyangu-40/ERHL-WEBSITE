ABOUT ERHL

Environmental restoration and Healthy living (ERHL) is a community based organisation founded by Peter Ndalahoma Banda, a student from the Malawi University of Science and Technology, studying Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering (Honours).

The idea of ERHL was developed after observing serious environmental and health challenges affecting communities, including deforestation, poor waste management, unsustainable agricultural practices and increasing cases of lifestyle diseases such as diabetes, cancer, heart disease and kidney failure.

These challenges inspired the creation of ERHL as a solution driven organisation focused on restoring the environment and promoting healthier lifestyles.
