Who we are

Environmental restoration and Healthy living (ERHL) is a community based organisation founded by peter Ndalahoma banda a student from the Malawi University of Science and Technology, studying bachelor of science in Chemical Engineering(honours).
The idea of ERHL was developed after observing serious enevironmental and health challenges affecting communities, including deforestation, poor waste management, unsustainable agricultural practices and increasing cases of lifestylke diseases such as diabetes, cancer,heart disease and kidney failure These challenges inspired the creation of ERHL as a solution driven organisation focused on restoring the environment and promoting healthier lifestyles

Mission

1
Restore and protect the environment

Promote activities like tree planting, conservation, and ecosystem restoration.

2
Promote sustainable practices

Encourage the use of clean energy, organic agriculture, and responsible waste management.

3
Empower communities

Educate and engage communities to take part in environmental protection and healthy living initiatives.

4
Encourage healthy lifestyles

Advocate for proper nutrition, regular exercise, and reduced consumption of processed foods.

Vision

  1. To build a sustainable society where communities live healthy lives in harmony with a restored and protected environment.
  2. To create a future where environmental restoration and healthy living practices are embraced by every community.
