About Us

Environmental Restoration and Healthy Living (ERHL) is dedicated to restoring the natural environment and promoting healthy lifestyles through sustainable practices, organic agriculture, clean energy initiatives, and community education programs.

We believe that environmental health and human health are interconnected. Our work focuses on creating sustainable solutions that benefit both the planet and its inhabitants.

For over 5 years, we've been working on the frontlines of environmental conservation, partnering with communities to create lasting positive change. Our integrated approach combines environmental restoration with health advocacy to ensure holistic benefits for people and planet.

Featured Projects

Explore some of our active environmental and health initiatives

Our clean energy solutions focus on transitioning communities to renewable power sources. We install solar panels, conduct energy efficiency audits, and provide training on sustainable energy management.

We implement comprehensive waste management programs that include collection, sorting, and recycling initiatives. Our goal is to reduce landfill waste by 75% in partner communities.

Through our tree planting campaigns, we've planted over 10,000 trees in the past three years. Each tree helps combat climate change and restore local ecosystems.

Our health promotion initiatives focus on preventative care, health education, and community wellness programs. We provide free health screenings and promote active lifestyles in underserved communities.
